2019-2021 | Fleet Farm

Sales Floor Representative

At Fleet Farm much of my experience from previous jobs carried over, primarily the ability to lift heavy items and to professionally communicate with others. A lot of my work consisted of assessing the scope of work for the day, communicating what I planned on doing to help accomplish our goals for our department, department mastery and memorization, and constant priority evaluation. Priority evaluation was also critical because at any given time there were multiple genuinely important jobs that could be done, and the focus needed to be given to the most critical jobs first. Similarly to the previously listed jobs, my communications skills vastly improved, as well as problem solving, interpersonal communication, customer service, and time management.
